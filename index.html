How it works

Three taps to a crowd-picked playlist

No accounts for guests, no fiddly setup. Start a session and hand the party to everyone in the room.

1

Host a session

Pick any Apple Music playlist or your whole library. Jukebox spins up a live session with a shareable 6-digit code and QR.

2

Guests join & vote

Friends join by code, by scanning the QR, or simply by being nearby — no account needed. They vote on songs and request their own.

3

The room decides

The most-voted track plays next. Anyone can vote to skip. The music stays fair, fun, and social all night long.

FAQ

Good questions

How do guests join a session?

Three ways: enter the 6-digit session code, scan the host's QR code, or — uniquely to Jukebox — just be nearby and join automatically. Guests don't need to create an account.

Do guests need Apple Music to vote?

No. Only the host needs Apple Music to play the audio. Guests can browse, vote, and request songs without any music subscription at all.

Is Jukebox free?

Yes — Jukebox is free to host and free for guests. An optional Premium subscription removes the session time limit for longer parties.

Can guests request songs that aren't in the playlist?

Absolutely. Guests can search the full catalog and submit a request. The host can auto-approve requests or approve them one by one.

What music service does it use?

Jukebox plays through Apple Music on the host's device, with full background playback, CarPlay, AirPlay, and lock-screen controls.

How many people can join?

Sessions are built for real parties — the whole room can join and vote in real time, with live vote counts updating instantly for everyone.
